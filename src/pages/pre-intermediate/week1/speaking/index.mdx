# Conversation questions

- How do you like to start your day?
- What's your favourite fruit?
- Do you enjoy cooking at home?
- How often do you exercise each week?
- What's your favourite outdoor activity?
- Have you ever tried meditation?
- What book are you currently reading?
- Do you play any sports?
- How do you unwind before bed?
- What's your favourite healthy snack?
- Have you ever grown your own food?
- How much water do you drink daily?
- Do you prefer tea or coffee?
- What's your favourite way to relax?
- Do you have a hobby that keeps you busy?
- How often do you visit the doctor for check-ups?
- What's your go-to healthy meal?
- Have you tried any new exercises lately?
- What does being healthy mean to you?
- Do you prefer working out at home or at the gym?
- How do you stay motivated to keep a healthy lifestyle?
- What's your favourite season and why?
- Have you ever taken a cooking class?
- What's the last movie you saw and loved?
- Do you prefer biking or walking?
- Have you ever participated in a marathon?
- How do you deal with stress?
- What's your favourite leisure activity?
- Do you make time for breakfast in the morning?
- How do you balance work and leisure?
- Do you follow any diet?
- What's your strategy for staying hydrated?
- Have you tried any fitness challenges?
- What hobbies help you relax?
- How do you stay active during the day?
- What's your favourite way to spend time outdoors?
- Do you have any tips for eating healthy on a budget?
- How do you approach setting health goals?
- Do you prefer individual or team sports?
- What's your favourite type of exercise equipment?
- How do you incorporate fruits and vegetables into your diet?
- Have you ever tried a digital detox?
- What's your favourite health-focused app?
- How do you make exercise fun?
- Do you have a regular sleep schedule?
- How do you make healthy food choices?
- What's your approach to mental wellness?
- Do you have any plant-based meals you enjoy?
- How do you stay fit without a gym membership?
- What's your favourite way to stay active with friends or family?
