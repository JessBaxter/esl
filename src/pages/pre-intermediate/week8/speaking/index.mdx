# Conversation questions

- Do you use a smartphone?
- What do you use your smartphone for?
- If you forget your phone at home, how do you feel?
- What's your favourite app on your phone?
- How often do you check your emails?
- What's your favourite website?
- How do you use technology to communicate with your friends?
- Do you think technology has made our lives easier?
- What's your opinion about social media?
- Can you live without the internet?
- Do you think it's important to learn how to use computers?
- What's the most useful piece of technology in your home?
- How often do you watch TV?
- Do you prefer reading books or e-books?
- What do you think about online shopping?
- How do you protect your personal information online?
- What's your favourite video game?
- Do you think robots will replace humans in some jobs?
- What's the most advanced piece of technology you own?
- Would you like to have a self-driving car?
- How do you feel about wearable technology like smartwatches?
- Have you ever used virtual reality technology?
- Do you think technology can solve environmental problems?
- What do you think about artificial intelligence?
- If you could invent a new piece of technology, what would it be?
- How do you feel about online privacy?
- Do you think technology addiction is a problem?
- What's your opinion on online education?
- How do you think technology has changed the way we work?
- Do you think technology can improve healthcare?
- Would you like to live in a smart home?
- What's your opinion on cryptocurrency?
- How do you think technology will change in the future?
- If you could create a new social media platform, what would it be like?
- Do you think it's important for children to learn coding?
- How do you think technology has changed the way we travel?
- Would you like to travel to space if given the opportunity?
- What's your opinion on 3D printing?
- Do you think technology has improved or worsened our relationships?
- How do you think technology can help people with disabilities?
- Would you like to try living in a virtual reality world?
- What's your opinion on online dating?
- Do you think technology can make us lazy?
- How do you think technology has changed entertainment?
- What's your opinion on drones?
- Do you think technology has made us more or less productive?
- What's your opinion on renewable energy technology?
