# Conversation questions

- What do you do to help the environment?
- Do you recycle at home?
- Have you ever planted a tree?
- How often do you use public transportation?
- Do you turn off lights when you leave a room?
- Have you ever participated in a beach cleanup?
- What do you think is the biggest environmental problem today?
- Do you try to save water?
- Have you ever visited a national park?
- Do you use reusable bags when you go shopping?
- Have you ever composted food scraps?
- What can we do to reduce air pollution?
- Do you think climate change is a serious issue?
- Have you ever used solar energy?
- How do you feel about using less plastic?
- Do you enjoy spending time outdoors in nature?
- Have you ever seen an endangered animal?
- Do you think cars are harmful to the environment?
- How can we protect forests?
- Do you use energy-saving light bulbs?
- Have you ever been on a nature hike?
- What do you think about wind energy?
- Do you try to buy products with eco-friendly packaging?
- Have you ever visited a recycling plant?
- How do you feel about using less paper?
- Do you think it's important to conserve energy?
- Have you ever participated in an environmental campaign?
- What do you think about electric cars?
- Do you know any endangered species?
- How do you feel about eating less meat for the environment?
- Do you think it's important to protect oceans?
- Have you ever volunteered for an environmental organization?
- What do you think about using bicycles as a mode of transport?
- Do you try to reduce your carbon footprint?
- Have you ever seen a renewable energy source in action?
- How do you feel about using less water?
- Do you think it's important to educate others about the environment?
- Have you ever visited a wildlife sanctuary?
- What do you think about recycling electronic waste?
- Do you support the idea of community gardens?
- Have you ever participated in an Earth Day event?
- How do you feel about using eco-friendly cleaning products?
- Do you think it's important to protect endangered habitats?
- Have you ever visited a botanical garden?
- What do you think about the impact of deforestation?
- Do you try to buy locally produced food?
- Have you ever taken part in a protest for environmental issues?
- How do you feel about using less fossil fuels?
- Do you think it's important to preserve natural landscapes?
- Have you ever read about environmental conservation efforts?
