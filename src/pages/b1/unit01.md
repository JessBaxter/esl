## Speaking ðŸ—¨

Feel free to use the following conversations questions to get you started:

- What makes a good friendship last a long time?
- How do relationships with family change as people grow older?
- What is one important rule for having a good relationship with classmates or coworkers?
- How can people keep in touch with friends who live far away?
- Do you think online friendships can be as strong as face-to-face friendships? Why or why not?
- What problems can happen when people do not communicate clearly?
- How can people show respect in different kinds of relationships?
- Who do people usually talk to when they need advice, and why?
- What is the difference between a close friend and an ordinary friend?
- How can people fix a relationship after an argument?
