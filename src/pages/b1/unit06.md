## Speaking ðŸ—¨

Feel free to use the following conversations questions to get you started:

- What was your first job, and what did you learn from it?
- How do you usually find out about job opportunities?
- Do you think it's important to enjoy your work? Why or why not?
- What kind of skills do you think are essential for a good employee?
- Have you ever worked in a team? What was your role, and how did you feel about it?
- What are some advantages of working from home compared to working in an office?
- How do you manage your time when you have a lot of tasks to complete at work or school?
- What is the most challenging part of your current or past job?
- Would you prefer to have a job with regular hours or a job with flexible hours? Why?
- Do you think technology has made work easier or more difficult? Can you give an example?
