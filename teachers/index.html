Classroom activities for an adult ELICOS context

Level: Any

TPR: Vocab Practice

Materials: Definitions of target words on pieces of paper (1 per student).
Time: 15+ minutes
Demo: YouTube video

  1. Assign each student in your class a different vocabulary word
  2. Have each student quickly learn the definition to their word
  3. Have students form one large circle so they are all side by side
  4. Start with the first student and have them loudly say their vocabulary word along with a quick motion for the word
  5. After the student performs the action while saying the word, have the rest of the students repeat the word and motion together
  6. Repeat until every student has given their word and motion
  7. Go around the circle one more time for practice
  8. The fun part: One student does their word+motion, then someone else's. That person must do their own word+motion, then someone else's. Students must remember each other's words and motions as quickly as possible.

Level: Any

Word Grab Race

Materials: Target words on cards.
Time: 15+ minutes

  1. Attach the vocab cards to a whiteboard, or spread them out over a desk.
  2. Divide the class into teams.
  3. The teacher says a word and the chosen student from each team runs to the front and grabs the correct card. Repeat until all cards have been nabbed. The team with the most at the end is the winner.

Level: Pre-Int to Advanced

Whispers

Time: 5+ minutes

  1. Have the students stand or sit in a row
  2. Person 1 whispers a sentence to person 2, without anyone else hearing
  3. Person 2 then needs to whisper the message to person 3 etc until it reaches the end of the line
  4. The last person then reveals the message to the class

Level: Any

I went to the supermarket...

Time: 5+ minutes

  1. Have the students stand or sit in a circle
  2. Person 1 starts by saying, 'I went to the supermarket and bought [ some bread ].'
  3. Person 2 then says, 'I went to the supermarket and bought some bread and [an apple].'
  4. Person 3 then says, 'I went to the supermarket and bought some bread , an apple and [some cheese].'
  5. Continue around the circle until someone is unable to remember all of the list.

Level: Int to Advanced

Synonyms/Antonyms

Time: 5+ minutes

  1. Say a word and point to a student. That student then gives a synonym for that word. Repeat for all students.
  2. Variation: Have the student give an antonym for the word
  3. Variation: The students take turns asking each other
  4. Variation: Add a 5 second time limit
