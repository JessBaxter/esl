50 Conversation Questions

Here are 50 conversation questions for you to practice speaking in class. You can use these questions to practice speaking with your classmates or with your teacher.

  1. What type of transport do you use every day?
  2. How often do you take the bus?
  3. Do you prefer travelling by train or by bus?
  4. What is the fastest way to travel in your city?
  5. How do you get to work or school?
  6. Have you ever ridden a horse?
  7. Can you ride a bicycle?
  8. Do you need a ticket to ride the underground?
  9. What colour is your bicycle?
  10. Who taught you to drive?
  11. How much does a bus ticket cost?
  12. What's the longest journey you have ever taken?
  13. Do cars in your country drive on the left or the right?
  14. How do you feel about flying?
  15. What is your favourite type of transport and why?
  16. Do you know how to change a tyre?
  17. What do you do if the bus is late?
  18. Is it safe to cycle in your city?
  19. How often do you use public transport?
  20. Do you wear a helmet when you ride a bike?
  21. Have you ever travelled by boat?
  22. What should you do if you get lost using public transport?
  23. Have you ever missed a train?
  24. Which is cheaper, a bus or a train ticket?
  25. How long does it take to travel from your home to the nearest city?
  26. Can you name three types of public transport?
  27. Do you have a favourite bus route?
  28. When is the next bus arriving?
  29. Are pets allowed on public transport?
  30. Have you ever taken a taxi?
  31. What do you think of electric cars?
  32. How do you find out the time of the last train?
  33. Do you think bicycles are a good way to get around?
  34. What was the last place you visited by train?
  35. Why do some people prefer walking?
  36. What is the busiest time for public transport in your area?
  37. Do you think transport is expensive?
  38. How can you save money on transport?
  39. What is the best seat on a bus?
  40. Do you need a licence to drive a scooter?
  41. Have you ever been on a double-decker bus?
  42. What do you do on long bus journeys?
  43. Do you like to sit by the window or the aisle on a train?
  44. What are the rules for bicycles in your town?
  45. How often do you fill up your car with petrol?
  46. Have you ever had a flat tyre?
  47. What do you think of self-driving cars?
  48. Have you ever used a map to find your way?
  49. What is the maximum speed limit in your city?
  50. Do you prefer travelling alone or with others?
