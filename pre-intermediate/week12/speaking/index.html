Conversation questions

  • What is your favourite season and why?
  • Do you like to spend time in nature? What do you enjoy doing?
  • Have you ever been to a national park? What did you see there?
  • What is your favourite animal and why do you like it?
  • Do you prefer mountains or the beach? Why?
  • What is the most beautiful place you have visited in nature?
  • Can you name a tree that you like? What does it look like?
  • Do you enjoy hiking or walking in the park? Why?
  • Have you ever seen a rainbow? When was it?
  • What is your favourite flower? Why do you like it?
  • Do you prefer warm weather or cold weather? Why?
  • Have you ever gone camping? What was your experience like?
  • What do you think is the most important thing to protect in nature?
  • Can you name a bird that you see often? What does it look like?
  • What is your favourite outdoor activity? Why do you enjoy it?
  • Have you ever seen a wild animal? What was it?
  • Do you like gardening? What do you grow?
  • What do you think about recycling? How can it help nature?
  • Can you describe a beautiful sunset you have seen?
  • Do you think people should spend more time in nature? Why?
  • What is your favourite natural place in your city or town?
  • How does nature make you feel? Why?
  • Can you name a natural disaster? What do you know about it?
  • Do you like to take photos of nature? What do you like to capture?
  • What is your favourite outdoor memory from childhood?
  • Do you prefer sunny days or rainy days? Why?
  • How can we help animals in danger?
  • What do you think is the most interesting plant? Why?
  • Have you ever participated in a clean-up event in nature?
  • Can you think of a movie that has beautiful nature scenes?
  • What do you know about the climate in your country?
  • Do you like to go for picnics? Where do you like to go?
  • How does nature change with the seasons?
  • Can you describe your dream nature vacation?
  • What sounds do you hear in nature? Which ones do you like?
  • Have you ever tried fishing? What was that like?
  • What do you think is the most beautiful animal? Why?
  • Do you know any traditional songs about nature?
  • How do you feel when you see a lot of trees?
  • Can you describe a time when you felt close to nature?
  • Do you think it is important to learn about nature in school? Why?
  • What is your favourite thing to do in the forest?
  • Have you ever seen the stars clearly? Where were you?
  • What is one thing you want to learn about nature?
  • How can we make our cities greener?
  • Do you like to watch documentaries about nature?
  • What is your favourite beach activity?
  • Have you ever gone birdwatching? What did you see?
  • What do you think is the biggest problem facing nature today?
  • Can you share a fun fact about a natural place or animal?
